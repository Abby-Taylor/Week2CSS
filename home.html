Fun Facts about Otters—

Did you know that otters hold hands while they sleep to prevent from floating away from one another?

Another way otters prevent themselves from floating away is to wrap themselves in giant kelp.

A group of otters is called a raft.


What do sea otters eat?

Sea otters eat a variety of hard-shelled invertebrates including the following most common:

  • Sea Urchins
  • Clams
  • Mussels
  • Crabs
